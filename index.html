Pizza is awesome
Pizza really is an amazing food. There are many different toppings you can put on pizza. All of the toppings you can put on pizza make the pizza taste delicious.
Pizza Toppings
You cannot go wrong with a classic pepperoni pizza. Everyone has had it and knows how good it is.
Sausage pizza: another classic topping that always hits the spot. In my personal opinion, it is second best to pepperoni.
Is cheese itself a topping or rather a core part of a pizza? No matter the answer to this question, we can all agree cheese pizza is awesome.
Supreme pizza: for those that cannot decide on a single topping. A wise choice to get all of them so you can have all the taste in a single slice.
Pizza is quite possibly mankind's greatest invention. A food that is enjoyed by billions worldwide is a testament to humanity's ingenuity and its neverending strive for perfection.
